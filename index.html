About Global Technologies

The Global Tech Story

Global Tech was established in 2020 with a clear mission to deliver reliable, innovative, and affordable CCTV and security solutions for homes, businesses, and industries. What started as a local initiative in Gangavathi has grown into a trusted security brand serving customers across the Koppal district and beyond.

We are authorized partners and distributors of leading security brands, managing over 2000+ completed installations and servicing 500+ satisfied clients.
